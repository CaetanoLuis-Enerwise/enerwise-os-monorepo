# Enerwise Enterprise Due Diligence Checklist

## Corporate and Legal

- Legal entity and contracting authority.
- Intellectual-property ownership assignments.
- Data processing agreement and privacy review.
- Customer data rights and retention schedule.
- Insurance requirements.
- Export control and sanctions screening where applicable.

## Software Supply Chain

- Private source repository.
- Protected branches and mandatory review.
- Reproducible build.
- Dependency inventory and software bill of materials.
- Open-source license review.
- Secret scanning.
- Vulnerability scanning and remediation policy.
- Signed releases and rollback artefacts.

## Product Evidence

- Versioned architecture.
- API specification.
- Automated test report.
- Benchmark code, assumptions, and outputs.
- Model artefact provenance and checksums.
- Known limitations and risk register.
- Release notes.

## Security

- Threat model.
- Identity and access design.
- Encryption design.
- Audit and logging design.
- Incident response plan.
- Backup and disaster recovery evidence.
- Penetration test before production.

## Operations

- SLO/SLA proposal.
- Monitoring and alert catalogue.
- On-call and escalation process.
- Site commissioning checklist.
- Emergency stop and fallback procedure.
- Change and release management.
- Customer support model.

## Hardware Control

- Supported device matrix.
- Protocol and firmware versions.
- Hardware-in-the-loop results.
- Command acknowledgement tests.
- Safety envelope approved by the asset owner.
- Electrical engineer/site operator sign-off.
- Evidence of rollback and local-controller recovery.

## Procurement Package

- Executive one-pager.
- Pilot statement of work.
- Commercial proposal.
- Security questionnaire response.
- Architecture diagram.
- Insurance and legal documents.
- References or pilot evidence when available.
